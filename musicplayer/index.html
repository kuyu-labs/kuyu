3. Data Deletion Policy

Under Google Play’s Data Safety policies, we provide users with the ability to request the deletion of their data. Because Kuyu Music Player: Radio & Cut does not collect personal information, maintain user accounts, or store your data on external servers, there is no personally identifiable data to delete from our systems.

However, if you wish to clear the anonymous usage data, local preferences, playlists, and settings associated with your current installation, you may do so at any time by following these steps:

  • Open your device Settings.
  • Navigate to Apps > Kuyu Music Player: Radio & Cut.
  • Select Storage & Cache and tap Clear Data.

Alternatively, completely uninstalling the application will immediately remove all locally stored data, preferences, and edits associated with the App.
