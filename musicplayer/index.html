Privacy Policy for Kuyu Music Player

Effective Date: May 3, 2026

1. Introduction

Thank you for using "Kuyu Music Player" (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our App. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect and Process

Microphone Access (RECORD_AUDIO)

  • Purpose: The App requests microphone access solely to power the "Adaptive Environment EQ" feature. This feature listens to ambient background noise to dynamically adjust audio equalizer levels for an optimal listening experience.
  • Privacy Guarantee: Audio data is processed locally and in real-time on your device. We do not record, store, or transmit any audio data to our servers or any third parties.

Local Media Files

  • The App accesses your device's local storage to discover, organize, and play your music files. Your music library, including audio files, metadata, and album art, is processed locally on your device. We do not upload your personal music files to any external servers.

Non-Personal Information & Analytics

  • We may collect non-personal information, such as device information (model, operating system), app usage data, and aggregated statistics. This information is used to improve the App's performance and user experience.
  • We use privacy-focused analytics and crash reporting services (such as Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics) to understand how the App performs, identify bugs, and improve stability. This tracking is anonymous and does not involve the collection of personal identifiers or advertising IDs.
  • No Advertising Tracking: This App does not use third-party advertising networks (such as AdMob) and does not share your data for ad targeting.

Personal Information

  • We do not intentionally collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, address, or email address, unless you voluntarily provide it for specific purposes, such as contacting our support team.
  • In-App Purchase Information: When you make an in-app purchase to support the app or unlock premium features, your payment information is processed securely by Google Play Billing. We do not store your credit card or other payment details. Google's privacy policy applies to these transactions.

3. Use of Information

  • Microphone Data: Used strictly in real-time on your device to adjust audio equalization based on your environment.
  • Non-Personal Information: Used to analyze app usage, improve app functionality, and fix software crashes.
  • Personal Information: Any personal information you voluntarily provide (e.g., for support) will be used solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiries.
  • In-App Purchases: Used to manage and verify your premium supporter status.

4. Sharing of Information

Service Providers

  • We may engage third-party service providers (e.g., Google Firebase for Crashlytics) to assist us with app analytics and stability monitoring. These providers process data anonymously, are contractually obligated to protect your information, and use it only for the purposes we specify. We do not share your data with advertisers.

Legal Requirements

  • We may disclose information if required by law or in response to a valid legal request.

Business Transfers

  • In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information collected through the App. All sensitive processing, such as audio analysis for the Adaptive Environment EQ, occurs entirely and securely on your device.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete it.

7. In-App Purchases and Google Play Billing

In-app purchases are processed through Google Play Billing. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for information on how they handle your payment information.
