Privacy Policy for Kuyu Minesweeper Pro

Last Updated: April 30, 2026

This policy informs you of our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use Kuyu Minesweeper Pro (the "Application").

Privacy-First Commitment: We believe in minimal data collection. We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, precise locations, or any personal identifiers that can link your activity to your real-world identity.

1. Information Collection and Use

To improve the "Pro" experience and ensure game balance across our 10+ game modes, we use Google Firebase Analytics to collect 100% anonymous usage data.

  • Anonymous Usage Statistics: We track events such as game mode selection, difficulty level completion, and time taken to solve puzzles. This helps us understand which features our users enjoy most.
  • No Personal Identifiers: We have explicitly disabled the collection of Advertising IDs (AAID), device serial numbers, and IP addresses.
  • No Third-Party Advertising: The application remains strictly ad-free and does not share your activity with any advertising networks.

Game progress, such as high scores and custom settings, is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2. Data Deletion Policy

Under Google Play’s Data Safety policies, we provide users with the ability to request the deletion of their data. Because Kuyu Minesweeper Pro does not collect personal information or maintain user accounts, there is no personally identifiable data to delete from our systems.

However, if you wish to clear the anonymous usage data associated with your current installation, you may do so by following these steps:

  • Open your device Settings.
  • Navigate to Apps > Kuyu Minesweeper Pro.
  • Select Storage & Cache and tap Clear Data.
  • Alternatively, uninstalling the application will remove all locally stored data.

3. Service Providers

We use Google Firebase (an Alphabet Inc. service) to provide analytics. This service is configured to handle data in an aggregated, anonymous manner that does not identify individual users.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our Application is suitable for all ages. Because we do not collect personal information, we do not knowingly or unknowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If you believe any data has been sent to us in error, please contact us immediately.
